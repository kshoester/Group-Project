What is Active Transportation?


The Government of Canada defines active transportation as "using your own power to get from on place to another." This includes:
- walking
- biking
- skateboarding
- in-line skating or rollerblading
- jogging and running
- non-mechanized wheel chairing
- snowshoeing and cross-country skiing

Why is Active Transportation important?


Active transporation is beneficial on many different levels. It is good for our health, society, transporation system, environment, and economy. Active transportation gives us an opportunity to be physically active on a regular basis. It is also accessible to all Canadians and increases social interactions. Furthermore, active transportation reduces road congestion and therefore also contributes to reducing greenhouse gas emission. Another side benefit is that it saves money on gas and parking!
