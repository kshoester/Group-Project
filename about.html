Ease of Transportation Index

Number of Collisions

The number of collisions variable was created to determine the number of motor vehicle incidents that occurred with pedestrians and cyclists. This variable used data from the Motor Vehicle Collisions dataset, which includes all traffic collisions events where a person was either Killed or Seriously Injured (KSI) from 2006 to 2021. We decided to use data from the year 2019 to conduct our analysis.

Walk Score

Measures the walkability of a location based on urban morphology, programs or land use, population and intersection density.

Score from 0–100:
0–24 Heavily car-dependent
25–49 Car-dependent
50–69 Somewhat walkable
70–89 Very walkable
90–100 Walkers paradise

Bike Theft Rate

This variable was included to allow users to gain an understanding of the number of bike thefts that occur around their neighborhood and schools. This data was taken from the Neighbourhood Crime Rates dataset, specifically the columns 'BIKETHEFT_.'
